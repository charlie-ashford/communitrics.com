Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 04, 2024

At Communitrics, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

In the event our services contain links to third-party sites and services, please be aware that those sites and services have their own privacy policies. After following a link to any third-party content, you should read their posted Privacy Policy information about how they collect and use personal information.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to any of your activities after you leave our services.

1. Information Collection

We collect two types of information:

  • Voluntarily provided information: any information you knowingly and actively provide us when using our services and promotions.
  • Automatically collected information: any information automatically sent by your devices in the course of accessing our services.

2. Discord Server Information

When tracking a YouTube channel, we collect:

  • Your Discord server ID
  • The specified YouTube channel ID

This allows us to send updates to your server as needed.

3. YouTube Channel Data

For tracked YouTube channels, we collect:

  • Channel ID and name
  • Subscriber count

This data is used to display information and monitor subscriber changes.

4. Subscriber Metrics

We calculate subscribers per day, per hour, per minute, and per second using YouTube's data and our own records.

5. Data Sharing

Communitrics does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

6. Policy Updates

At our discretion, we may change our Privacy Policy to reflect updates to our business processes, current acceptable practices, or legislative/regulatory changes. If we change this Privacy Policy, we will post the changes here at the same link by which you are accessing this Privacy Policy.

If required by law, we will get your permission or give you the opportunity to opt in to or opt out of, as applicable, any new uses of your personal information.

7. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

8. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or questions, reach out to us at:

  • Email: admin@communitrics.com
  • Discord: charlieashford
